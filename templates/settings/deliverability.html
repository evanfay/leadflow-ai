Deliverability Best Practices

Warm up new accounts

Start at 10 emails/day and ramp up over 4 weeks. Enable "Warmup Mode" on any new account in Email Accounts settings.

Send in business hours

LeadFlow automatically restricts sends to 8–11am and 1–4pm on weekdays. Never send at off-hours to business inboxes.

Keep emails short

Under 100 words, no images, no attachments in cold email. Plain text reads as human. Spam filters flag HTML-heavy emails.

Honor unsubscribes immediately

LeadFlow auto-adds any unsubscribe reply to your Do Not Contact list and stops all future sends to that address.

Set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC

Configure these DNS records for your domain. Gmail/Google Workspace walks you through it. Without them, you'll land in spam.

Rotate sending accounts

If you're sending at volume, connect 2–3 accounts. LeadFlow automatically rotates them round-robin so no single account gets flagged.
