How to write dynamic & personalized content
Personalization variables:
Click a tag to copy it, then paste into the subject or body. Or click inside the body first, then click a tag to insert it directly.
{% for code, desc in [ ('{{first_name}}', "first name"), ('{{last_name}}', "last name"), ('{{company}}', 'company'), ('{{signal_1}}', 'signal 1'), ('{{signal_2}}', 'signal 2'), ('{{calendly_link}}', 'booking link'), ] %} {% endfor %}
Use [square brackets] for AI instructions:
Anything in brackets is a prompt to the AI — it writes that part based on the lead's data. The rest of the email stays exactly as you typed it.
[1-sentence observation about {{company}}] [relevant pain point for their industry] [one short case study result] [write a curiosity-gap subject line]
Use {{company}} / {{first_name}} for personalization, or put [AI instruction] to let it write one.
Mix real data ({{company}}) with AI instructions ([write a personalized hook]) freely — the AI fills in the brackets and keeps the fixed text as-is.
